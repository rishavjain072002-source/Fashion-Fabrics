Meet The Owner

Nirmala Jain

Nirmala Jain is a dedicated and hardworking woman who has nurtured Fashion & Fabrics with passion and precision. She treats this boutique like her own child, pouring her heart and soul into every detail. As the sole creative force behind everything this boutique stands for, Nirmala single-handedly manages every aspect of the business—from curating exquisite fabrics to crafting bespoke designs and ensuring exceptional customer service.

With unwavering commitment and tireless dedication, she works tirelessly to bring fashion dreams to life. Her expertise, creativity, and personal touch have made Fashion & Fabrics a trusted name in the industry. Every piece that leaves our boutique carries her vision of elegance, quality, and craftsmanship. Nirmala's passion for fashion and her commitment to excellence is what truly sets Fashion & Fabrics apart.
