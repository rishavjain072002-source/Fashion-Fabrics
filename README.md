# 🌟 Fashion & Fabrics - Boutique Website

A modern, elegant, and fully responsive website for Fashion & Fabrics boutique.

## ✨ Features

- **Modern Design**: Clean, elegant UI with your signature color scheme
- **Responsive Layout**: Perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Navigation Menu**: Easy access to all sections
- **About Section**: Showcase your boutique with location, contact info, and photo
- **Gallery**: Display your work and designs
- **Testimonials**: Feature customer reviews and ratings
- **Appointment Booking**: Customers can book appointments with:
  - Name
  - Phone Number
  - Email
  - Date Selection
  - Time Slot Selection
  - Service Type
  - Additional Notes
- **Google Forms Integration**: Auto-sync appointments to Google Forms/Sheets
- **Local Storage**: Automatic backup of all bookings
- **Contact Section**: Multiple ways to reach you

## 🎨 Design Features

- **Color Scheme**:
  - Primary: `#EDE8D0` (Cream)
  - Text: `#573310` (Brown)
  - Accents and variations included

- **Typography**: Professional, readable fonts
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions and hover effects
- **Icons**: Font Awesome icons for visual enhancement

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Download/Clone** the repository
2. **Open** `index.html` in your browser
3. **Customize** with your business info

## ⚙️ Configuration

### Basic Customization

Edit `index.html` to update:
- Business location and contact info
- About section text
- Boutique photo
- Gallery images
- Testimonials

### Google Forms Setup

To receive appointment bookings automatically:

1. Create a Google Form with these fields:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Email Address
   - Preferred Date
   - Time Slot
   - Service Required
   - Additional Notes

2. Get your Form ID and field entry IDs
3. Update `script.js` with your IDs
